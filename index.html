About Notes Hub

Notes Hub is a simple yet powerful place where I collect and share my learning notes from Web Development and Blockchain. This website is built entirely using HTML only, as part of a special hackathon challenge.

The Goal of Notes Hub

  • Provide easy-to-understand notes for beginners
  • Share step-by-step concepts of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Help learners with examples and quick revision
  • Document my own journey as I grow in Web Development & AI Engineering

Why I Created Notes Hub?

I started Notes Hub to organize my notes from Rohit Negi Bhaiya’s classes and make them accessible in one place. While preparing for my career in AI and Web Development, I realized that structured notes not only help me revise but can also help other learners who are on the same path.

My Vision

My vision with Notes Hub is to keep expanding it beyond HTML & CSS, and eventually include:

  • JavaScript Notes
  • Blockchain Notes
  • AI & Data Science Notes

This way, Notes Hub can become a complete learning companion for anyone passionate about coding and technology.
